Crow

A crow can remember and distinguish human faces during his entire life.

  • Scientific Name: Corvus brachyrhynchos
  • Average Length: 40-53 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Habitat: open areas with few trees.

Extremely intelligents, crows are capable of solving logical problems with more than 3 puzzles assembled together. Due to it's incredible memory and logical thinking, crows have a great adaptative behavior, learning how to use the environment in it's own favour.
